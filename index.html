How to Play

Learn to solve maths using BODMAS order of operations!

  1. 1 Solve Brackets (B) first — expressions inside ( ) have highest priority.
  2. 2 Next tackle Orders (O) — powers like 2³ and square roots √.
  3. 3 Division (D) and Multiplication (M) — solve both left to right.
  4. 4 Addition (A) and Subtraction (S) — solve both left to right.
  5. 5 Select the correct answer from the four options given.
  6. 6 Click Submit to check your answer and earn stars!
